    ABOUT

    The Yardenit community
    supports many projects

    It is important for us to support Christian projects and communities in Israel. We want to turn the Jordanian community into a vibrant and active community whose goal is to encourage pilgrims to come to the Holy Land, to be immersed in Jordan, to get to know the Sea of ​​Galilee and the holy sites. Connect to Jesus, to the values ​​of peace, brotherhood, love and giving.

    We purchase products for sale in our store from Christian trainers and creators

    We guard the Jordan, clean and equip baptismal and prayer sites along it for the benefit of the pilgrims who come to the Holy Land.

    We host people with disabilities on the site

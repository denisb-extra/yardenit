We are very excited to share with you our latest project:

Your personal fingerprint in the Holy Land

The Jordan River Living Stones is a wall made of personal ceramic tiles, installed one by one, creating an uplifting presentation from our dear friends of Yardenit all around the globe.

Each tile carries the name of the donor: a Pastor, a Church, an organization, or any believer who wishes to leave a fingerprint in the Holy Land.

The Jordan River Living Stones

wall is located at our Olive Garden and will lead into the ‘Jordan River Way’ promenade.

We welcome you to participate. The cost of the personal tile is $250.

After placing your order (see below) we will produce your customized tile. Once it is ready (up to 30 business days), we at Yardenit will add it to the Jordan River Living stones wall.

A photo and a certificate copy of your Living Stone will be emailed to you immediately. The actual certificate will be sent to your Home/Office address by registered mail.

Planning on visiting Yardenit in the near future?

Your Living Stone will be ready and waiting for you to install it by your own (please advise).
